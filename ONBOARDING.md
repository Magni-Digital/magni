# How Magni works

**The short version:** every morning the tool hands you a fresh set of small
practices that have a genuinely weak website — each with one true thing wrong and
a first line already drafted. You confirm it, put it in your words, and send it
yourself. **The tool finds and checks; you write and send.**

## Where the leads come from
- **Already loaded:** a few hundred qualified practices are in the tool right now —
  enough to work for a couple of weeks without adding anything.
- **Adding more:** paste new finds into the **Magni import Google Sheet** (one row
  per practice — at minimum a company name and a website). That sheet is your
  inbox. Overnight, the tool reads it, checks each new site, and adds the good
  ones to your queue. No files, no software.

## What the tool does to each practice (automatically, overnight)
1. **Opens their website** and runs objective checks — no HTTPS, not
   mobile-friendly, slow, an outdated copyright year, a thin/placeholder page, no
   way to book, dead links.
2. **Keeps only the ones with a real, checked weakness.** Clean sites are dropped.
3. **Drafts one true opening line** about that weakness — and it can only say
   things it actually verified, so it never makes something up.
4. **Grabs the contact email** off the site (and flags whether it looks valid).

## Your two tabs
- **Today's queue** — the practices that qualified, each with its findings + a
  drafted line. This is your daily work.
- **Research · no site** — practices it couldn't find a website for. Open their
  LinkedIn; if you find a site, paste it in the box on the card and it'll get
  checked. (No website at all is a strong opener too.)

Use the **filters** (vertical, city, has-email) to batch similar outreach.

## Working a card
1. Read the **checked findings** — the true things wrong with their site.
2. Read the **draft line**, then **open their site and confirm it's actually
   true** — and rewrite it in your own voice. *The draft is a running start; your
   rewrite is the work.*
3. **Copy** it (and the email), paste into your own email, and **send it
   yourself.** The tool never emails anyone.
4. Hit **Mark sent** (or **Skip**).
5. At the end, hit **Download CSV** — your day's work (your lines, your notes, who
   you sent to) exports in the spreadsheet's columns. Paste into the Magni CRM
   sheet. **That sheet is your record.**

## Worth knowing
- **You won't lose leads.** Sent ones drop off for good; skipped ones come back in
  30 days; anything you don't get to stays and comes back next time.
- **Notes** live on each card (and ride along in your CSV). **"More info"** shows
  size, industry, revenue, LinkedIn, etc.
- **It refreshes itself every morning** — you just open the page. Nothing to
  install, nothing to run.

**The one rule:** the tool finds the practice, checks the site, and drafts the
line — **you always confirm and send.** It never reaches out to anyone on its own.
